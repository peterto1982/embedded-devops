Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, process, and expertise.

What industries do you specialize in?
We specialize in embedded systems across various industries including automotive, industrial IoT, consumer electronics, medical devices, and telecommunications. Our expertise is specifically tailored to the unique challenges of embedded software delivery, hardware-software integration, and real-time systems.
How long does a typical engagement take?
Engagement timelines vary based on project scope. Typically:
  • Discovery Phase: 1-2 weeks
  • Strategy Phase: 2-3 weeks
  • Implementation: 2-6 months (depending on complexity)
We work in focused sprints to deliver measurable value at each stage.
What is your approach to CI/CD pipeline design?
Our approach is tailored to your specific needs. We follow a proven methodology:
  • Analyze your current systems and pain points
  • Design a scalable pipeline architecture
  • Implement automation for build, test, and deploy
  • Integrate quality gates and security checks
  • Provide training and ongoing support
We focus on reliability, reproducibility, and maintainability.
Do you support migration from legacy systems?
Yes, absolutely. We specialize in legacy system modernization. We understand the challenges of transitioning from manual processes to automated CI/CD pipelines. Our approach minimizes risk by:
  • Maintaining backward compatibility during transition
  • Phased rollout to reduce disruption
  • Comprehensive testing at each stage
  • Team training and knowledge transfer
What tools and technologies do you work with?
We have extensive expertise across industry-leading tools:
  • CI/CD Platforms: Jenkins, GitLab CI, GitHub Actions
  • Containerization: Docker, Podman, Kubernetes
  • Build Systems: Yocto, Bitbake, CMake, Make
  • Testing: Unit testing frameworks, HIL automation, protocol testing
  • Code Quality: SonarQube, Coverity, MISRA compliance
  • Version Control: Git, Gerrit, GitLab
We also work with custom tools and frameworks specific to your environment.
How do you ensure code quality and security?
Code quality and security are integrated into every stage of our CI/CD pipelines:
  • Automated static analysis (SonarQube, Coverity)
  • Security vulnerability scanning
  • Code coverage tracking and enforcement
  • Merge quality gates that prevent low-quality code
  • Compliance checks (MISRA, industry standards)
  • Continuous monitoring and reporting
Can you help with hardware-in-the-loop (HIL) testing automation?
Yes, HIL automation is one of our core specialties. We help you:
  • Design and implement HIL test environments
  • Automate hardware flashing and testing
  • Integrate simulation with hardware testing
  • Create reusable test rigs and frameworks
  • Reduce manual testing time significantly
This is particularly valuable for embedded systems where hardware integration is critical.
Do you provide training and knowledge transfer?
Absolutely. Knowledge transfer and team enablement are essential parts of our engagement:
  • Hands-on training during implementation
  • Documentation and best practices guides
  • Team workshops and knowledge sessions
  • Ongoing support during transition period
  • Your team becomes self-sufficient and independent
We believe in empowering your team, not creating dependency.
How do you measure success?
We measure success through concrete metrics:
  • Reduced build and deployment time
  • Increased code quality scores
  • Reduced bug escape rate
  • Improved team productivity
  • Faster time-to-market
  • Higher system reliability
  • Reduced manual intervention
We establish baseline metrics at the start and track improvements throughout the engagement.
What is your pricing model?
We offer flexible engagement models tailored to your needs:
  • Project-based: Fixed scope and timeline
  • Sprint-based: Focused work in 2-4 week sprints
  • Retainer: Ongoing support and optimization
  • Hybrid: Combination of above models
We provide transparent pricing with no hidden fees. Contact us for a custom quote based on your specific requirements.
